# Product Analyst / Quality Assurance Portfolio

## Overview
This repository showcases my experience as a Product-aligned Quality Engineering professional, with a focus on product requirements, business acceptance testing (UAT), and release readiness in regulated digital environments.

The portfolio highlights how I approach:
- Translating business problems into clear requirements
- Defining acceptance criteria and test strategies
- Leading end-to-end UAT and release readiness
- Driving managed-to-self-service digital transformation

## Case Study: Self-Service Campaign Builder
This case study demonstrates the transformation of a managed business workflow into a controlled self-service (DIY) digital experience across Web and Mobile platforms.

### What This Case Study Covers
- Problem analysis and business opportunity
- DIY solution design with compliance guardrails
- Functional requirements and acceptance criteria
- UAT test planning and requirement traceability
- Executive-level release readiness assessment
- Business impact and governance considerations
